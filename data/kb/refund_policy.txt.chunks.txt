Refund Policy

1. Eligibility for Refund
- Customers are eligible for a refund if:
  - The product is damaged or defective.
  - The wrong item was delivered.
  - The order was not delivered within the promised timeframe.

2. Refund Process
- Refund requests must be initiated within 7 days of delivery.
- Agents must verify order details before initiating a refund.
- Once approved, refunds are processed within 5-7 business days.

3. Compensation for Delays
---CHUNK---
3. Compensation for Delays
- If delivery is delayed beyond 5 business days from the expected date:
  - Customers may be offered a partial refund or discount coupon.
  - Full refunds may be considered for extreme delays.

4. Non-Refundable Cases
- Products damaged due to misuse are not eligible.
- Refund requests beyond the allowed time window may be denied.
