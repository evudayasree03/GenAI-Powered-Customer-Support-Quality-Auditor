Customer Support Policy

1. Communication Guidelines

- Support agents must always maintain a polite, respectful, and empathetic tone.

- Agents should actively listen to the customer and acknowledge their concerns.

- Avoid using technical jargon unless necessary, and explain clearly when used.



2. Response Time

- Initial response to customer queries should be within 2 hours during business hours.

- Follow-ups should be provided within 24 hours if the issue is not resolved.
---CHUNK---

3. Handling Delayed Orders

- Agents must apologize for the inconvenience caused by delays.

- Clearly explain the reason for the delay if available.

- Provide an updated delivery timeline.

- Reassure the customer that the issue is being actively monitored.



4. Customer Satisfaction

- Ensure the customer feels heard and supported.

- Offer solutions rather than just explanations.

- Confirm with the customer if the issue has been resolved before closing the interaction.
