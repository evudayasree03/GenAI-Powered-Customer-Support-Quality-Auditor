Delivery Policy

1. Standard Delivery Timelines
- Orders are typically delivered within 3-5 business days.
- Delivery timelines may vary depending on location and product availability.

2. Delayed Deliveries
- Delays may occur due to:
  - Weather conditions
  - Logistics issues
  - High demand periods

3. Handling Delays
- Customers must be informed proactively if delays are expected.
- Agents should provide real-time tracking updates when available.
---CHUNK---
- Updated delivery timelines must be communicated clearly.

4. Extended Delays
- If delivery exceeds 5 business days beyond the estimated date:
  - The case should be flagged for priority handling.
  - Compensation options should be considered.

5. Failed Deliveries
- If delivery fails due to incorrect address or customer unavailability:
  - A reattempt should be scheduled.
  - The customer should be contacted for confirmation.
